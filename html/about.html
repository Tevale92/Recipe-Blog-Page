About Baker's Bliss

Welcome to Group 2's kitchen! We are a small group of friends living together in Montreal who share a passion for baking.


What started as a simple hobby has now grown into a love for experimenting with different flavors and techniques.


We constantly try to make new recipes and reimagine classic ones by tweaking ingredients, and adding our unique twists.


Our goal is to inspire fellow bakers and food lovers by sharing our creations.


Baking is more than just a hobby for us—it's a way to express our creativity, challenge ourselves,


and, most importantly, have fun together. We hope you enjoy our journey and try a few of our recipes for yourself!

How It Started

We met each other while studying at McGill University and were placed together in the same apartment in the McGill Ghetto.


As broke college students, we found ourselves cooking for each other and putting every last ingredient to good use.


Our shared kitchen experiments led us to create a fun tradition of naming a “Head Chef” for the night.


The "Head Chef" would plan the meal, lead the cooking, and share new tricks with the rest of the group.



Eventually, we started venturing into baking, making everything from cookies and muffins to our own versions of French pastries.


When our friends tasted our baked treats, they encouraged us to keep creating and even joked that we should open a bakery someday!


Since then, our passion for baking has only grown. We decided to document our journey online and share it with the world.


That's how Baker's Bliss was born,a place for us to share our love of baking and inspire others to create something delicious.
