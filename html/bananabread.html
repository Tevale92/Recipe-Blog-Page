Banana Bread

Posted on October 5, 2024 by Louis Chartier

Introduction

This classic recipe transforms overripe bananas into a moist, tender loaf that's perfect for any time of day. Whether you're an experienced baker or trying your hand at baking for the first time, this foolproof recipe will guide you to create a delicious treat that's sure to become a family favorite.

Recipe Overview

  • Preparation time: 15 minutes
  • Baking time: 60 minutes
  • Servings: 1 loaf (10-12 slices)
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups (about 4-5) mashed overripe bananas
  • 3/4 cup (150g) brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in mashed bananas and vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the banana mixture, stirring just until combined.
  6. Bake for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove from pan and cool completely on a wire rack.

Tips & Variations

Here are a few tips to make the recipe even better:

  • Use ripe bananas for the best flavor and natural sweetness.
  • Don't overmix the batter; stir just until ingredients are combined to ensure a tender bread.
  • Chocolate Chip: Add 1/2 cup chocolate chips to the batter.
