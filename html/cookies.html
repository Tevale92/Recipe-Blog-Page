The Best Chocolate Chip Cookie

Posted on October 5, 2024 by Louis Chartier

Introduction

Today, I’m excited to share the best recipe for chocolate chip cookies! I’ve been baking this recipe for years, and it never fails to impress. Anyone at any baking skill level can make the easy and delicious recipe.

Recipe Overview

  • Preparation time: 15 minutes
  • Baking time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 8-10
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened (2 sticks)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Optional: 1 cup chopped nuts (like walnuts or pecans)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add the eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract, and mix until combined.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving and enjoy!

Tips & Variations

Here are a few tips to make the recipe even better:

  • For a richer flavor, add 1/4 cup of dark chocolate chips.
  • Try swapping regular butter with salted butter for a savory twist.
