Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and grease a 12-cup muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Add oil, egg, and milk. Stir until just combined.
  4. Fold in the blueberries gently.
  5. Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Let the muffins cool for 10 minutes before serving.
